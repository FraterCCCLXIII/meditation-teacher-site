Common Questions

What should I bring to a retreat?

Comfortable clothing, any meditation props you prefer (we provide cushions/chairs), a journal, and an open mind. A detailed packing list will be sent upon registration.

Are beginners welcome?

Absolutely! While some meditation experience is helpful, all events include instruction suitable for beginners while offering depth for experienced practitioners.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refunds up to 30 days before, 50% refund 14-30 days before, no refunds within 14 days (but you may transfer your registration).
